## Honest limitations

This stack is the strongest free substitute available under the project's free-only constraint.
It is NOT equivalent to:
- Accredited SOC2 / ISO27001 audit
- NCC Group or similar professional pen-test
- Commercial signed binary attestation (Authenticode EV / Apple Developer ID)

Users with regulated procurement requirements should engage their own auditor
against the public repo at https://github.com/antonio-amore-akiki/amore.
